FAQs about Spit Roast Service in   Northern Beaches

Many customers planning an event on the Northern Beaches have questions about how spit roast catering works, what menu options are available, and how the service is organised for different types of events. Below are some common questions that help explain how this catering service operates for parties, weddings, corporate functions, and private celebrations across the Northern Beaches.

  • 1. How much does spit roast catering cost on the Northern Beaches?

    Spit roast catering on the Northern Beaches is priced from $40 per head for on-site staffed packages with 2 to 3 meats, 3 to 4 sides, carving service and disposables included. The final price depends on guest numbers, menu selections, service duration and any add-ons such as beverages, desserts or real crockery upgrades. Most caterers apply a small surcharge for groups under 50 guests because fixed costs for equipment, travel and staffing apply regardless of headcount. When requesting a quote, ask for a fully itemised breakdown rather than a single per-head figure so you can compare packages accurately.

  • 2. Do you travel to Palm Beach or Avalon for spit roast catering?

    Yes. Cater King services the full length of the Northern Beaches peninsula including Palm Beach, Avalon Beach, Newport and all suburbs south to Manly. A travel surcharge may apply for locations above Mona Vale depending on access and distance from our base. Confirm your venue suburb when requesting a quote and we will include any applicable travel costs in the itemised pricing.

  • 3. What is included in a spit roast catering package on the Northern Beaches?

    A standard on-site spit roast package includes 2 to 3 roasted meats, 3 to 4 sides (roasted potatoes, coleslaw, garden salad, pasta salad), bread rolls, condiments, an on-site chef for 2 hours of service, all rotisserie equipment, and disposable plates, cutlery and serviettes. Items not included at the base price typically include beverages, desserts, real crockery upgrades, tables and chairs, and any council permits for public space events. Always confirm the full inclusion list before accepting a quote.

  • 4. Can you cater a wedding on the Northern Beaches with a spit roast?

    Yes. Cater King caters weddings across the Northern Beaches including beachside receptions, backyard celebrations and formal venue events. Wedding spit roast packages include upgraded presentation, additional wait staff, real crockery options and coordination with your venue on timing and access. Wedding packages are priced above the standard on-site rate to reflect the additional coordination, staffing and service requirements. Book at least 2 to 3 weeks in advance for weddings; peak season weekends from October to March fill quickly.

  • 5. What is the minimum guest number for spit roast catering on the Northern Beaches?

    The minimum guest number for a standard spit roast catering package is 20 guests. Below this threshold, the fixed costs of equipment, travel and staffing make the per-head price significantly higher than the standard rate. For gatherings of 10 to 20 people, a simplified drop-off package or an alternative catering format may be a more practical option. Contact the team to discuss what works best for your group size.

  • 6. How far in advance should I book spit roast catering on the Northern Beaches?

    For small to medium events (under 80 guests), book at least 3 to 7 days in advance. For weddings and large events above 100 guests, 2 to 4 weeks notice is recommended. During peak periods (October to March, school holiday weekends, public holiday weekends), preferred dates fill quickly. Booking as early as possible once your event date and guest numbers are confirmed gives you the best access to date availability and menu customisation options.

  • 7. Can you cater outdoor events at Northern Beaches parks and reserves?

    Yes. Cater King caters outdoor events at parks, reserves and beachside locations across the Northern Beaches. Some public spaces require council permits for commercial food service and open flame cooking. Our team can advise on whether your preferred venue has restrictions and whether drop-off service (pre-cooked, no on-site flame) or on-site spit roasting is the appropriate option for your location. Confirm your venue address when booking so we can check access and any relevant council requirements.
